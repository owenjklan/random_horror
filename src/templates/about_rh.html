What is Random Horror?

Random Horror is intended as a fun way to spurr the creation of short and creative horror stories. Roll the dice to get random people, places and items and a plot device. Pick your favourite set of results to get writing.

Submitted stories can be rated and enjoyed by other users.

Why no comments section?

Honestly? Because the Internet doesn't need yet another vector for spammers and cowardly jerks to practice. Sometimes there simply doesn't need anything to be said.
